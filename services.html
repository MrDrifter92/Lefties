Upholstery & Lounges

We bring lounges back to life. Whether it’s fabric or leather, grubby arms or food-stained cushions — we deep clean, rinse properly, and leave it fresh.

What’s in the process?

  • Pre-check of fabric type (we clean natural & synthetic safely)
  • Vacuum and prep to remove hair, grit, and surface grime
  • Low-moisture pre-spray to soften oils, sweat, and stains
  • Rinsed via hot water extraction or safe low-moisture method
  • Drying pass to reduce moisture — ready within hours

What makes us different?

  • Local, not a franchise: We take the time to get it right — fewer jobs, better results.
  • Ethical operators: If a job’s not right for us, we refer it on. No BS.
  • Child and pet-safe: All products are professional-grade, non-toxic and residue-free once dry.
  • Optional Add-ons: Odour removal, sanitiser, or protection coating available on request.

How long does it take to dry?

Most upholstery is dry within 4–6 hours. Leather and synthetic blends dry quicker. We’ll leave the windows open or pop the fan on to help.

Need to know

  • We’re fully insured, police-checked and Blue Card accredited.
  • WoolSafe compatible products used on delicate fabrics.
  • No promises on impossible stains, but we’ll tell you upfront what can and can’t be done.
