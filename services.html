Upholstery & Lounges

We specialize in revitalizing your upholstery and lounges, bringing them back to their pristine condition. Whether your furniture is made of delicate fabric or durable leather, and regardless of whether it has grubby arms or food-stained cushions, we provide a deep clean. Our process ensures proper rinsing, leaving your upholstery fresh, hygienic, and looking its best.

What’s in the process?

  • Pre-check of fabric type (we clean natural & synthetic safely): Before we begin, our experts meticulously identify your upholstery's fabric type. This crucial step allows us to select the safest and most effective cleaning methods, ensuring that both natural and synthetic materials are treated with the utmost care and precision.
  • Vacuum and prep to remove hair, grit, and surface grime: We start with a thorough high-powered vacuuming to remove loose dirt, pet hair, dust, and other surface debris. This prepares the fabric for a deeper clean and prevents abrasive particles from being worked into the fibers during the cleaning process.
  • Low-moisture pre-spray to soften oils, sweat, and stains: A specialized low-moisture pre-treatment solution is gently applied to the fabric. This solution is designed to break down and emulsify tough oils, sweat, and stubborn stains, making them easier to extract without oversaturating your upholstery.
  • Rinsed via hot water extraction or safe low-moisture method: Depending on your fabric's specific needs, we employ either advanced hot water extraction for a deep rinse or a safe, low-moisture cleaning method. Both techniques are highly effective at flushing out dirt and cleaning solutions, leaving no sticky residue behind.
  • Drying pass to reduce moisture; ready within hours: After cleaning, we perform a dedicated drying pass to significantly reduce moisture in the fabric. This accelerates the drying time, ensuring your upholstery is ready for use within just a few hours, minimizing inconvenience.

How long does it take to dry?

Most upholstery is typically dry within 4 to 6 hours after our cleaning process. Factors like fabric type can influence this; for instance, leather and synthetic blends often dry even quicker. To assist with rapid drying, we will ensure windows are open for ventilation or strategically place a fan to promote air circulation.
