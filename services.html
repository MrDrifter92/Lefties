Our Revival Services

What we do, and how we do it right.

Carpet Revival

Daily life is tough on carpets. Dirt, dust, allergens, and mystery spots get ground in deep. Our Carpet Revival isn't a quick once-over. We use professional hot water extraction to flush out years of built-up grime from the base of the fibres.

  • Pre-inspection & commercial vacuum
  • Targeted pre-spray for oils and soil
  • Hot water extraction or low-moisture clean
  • Hand-applied spot treatment if needed
  • Carpet grooming and drying setup (fans if required)
  • Dry and ready by evening

Upholstery & Lounge Cleaning

Your couch is the centre of your home—and it cops a lot. We safely clean fabric and leather upholstery, identifying the material first to use the right method. We lift out body oils, food stains, and odours.

  • Pre-inspection of fabric or leather
  • Gentle pre-treatment and conditioning
  • Controlled hot rinse & vacuum extraction
  • Optional deodoriser or protectant
  • Dry within 4–6 hours

Stain & Odour Treatment

Red wine, coffee, pet urine — we've seen it all. Some stains are permanent, but if it can be moved, we’ve got the skills to do it.

  • Stain ID and assessment
  • Specialist chemical application
  • Enzyme cleaners for pet urine breakdown
  • Final rinse and inspection
  • Honest feedback on what's realistic

Rug & Mattress Revival

Delicate rugs and the mattress you sleep on deserve a deep hygienic clean. We remove allergens and odours, using safe, professional-grade tools.

  • Material-safe rug assessment
  • Hot rinse or dry clean method depending on rug type
  • Mattress extraction with allergen treatment
  • Safe for kids, pets, and sensitive skin
