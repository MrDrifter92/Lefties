About Lefties

Our foundation was built through years of leading operations and designing service systems for residential care settings, alongside years spent on the tools across building sites. We learnt the value of training apprentices, sharing skills, and lending a hand to neighbours and mates.

We are shaped by trial, error, the occasional stuff-up, and some impeccable wins. It’s also where we’ve built a deep respect for proper trades, skilled people, and the need to keep finding better ways to do things right.

After the burnout of COVID, we launched Lefties as a passion project, building a service model from the ground up.

We originally paused operations in 2023, even though the business was going strong in Brisbane. The timing just wasn’t right. Now, in 2025, we’re proud to officially bring Lefties back, this time in Hervey Bay and across the Fraser Coast.

We’re not here to cut corners, upsell you at every turn, or pretend we’re the biggest name in town.

We’re here to offer Hervey Bay a trusted, no-nonsense service, delivering expert carpet and upholstery cleaning, alongside solid home repair and maintenance work.

We don’t hide behind badges, associations, or corporate-sounding fluff. We let the work, and the people we work for, speak for itself.

💙 Our Values

These aren't wall art. They’re how we operate on every job, with every client.

True Empathy

We care about the home we walk into, and the people and pets in it. If it matters to you, it matters to us. Your dog especially matters to us. 😉

Community

We’re not just Hervey Bay locals—we act like it. We back our community, refer good operators who share our values, and celebrate the ones doing great work. There’s room for all of us when the job’s done right.

Transparency

We won’t upsell you. We won’t oversell ourselves, but we won’t undersell ourselves either. You’ll get the truth, the options, and the outcome. No dodgy surprises. We can bundle jobs, work with your budget, and still make it fair for both of us.

Quality

We take on fewer jobs per day so we can do them right. No shortcuts, no “in and out” cleans. That’s how we keep the standard high, and why people keep coming back.

When you choose Lefties, you’re not just supporting a small business, you’re backing honest trades built on real mentorship, skill, and pride in doing the job properly.
