Anti-Inflammatory Diet Tips

  • Eat leafy greens, berries, fatty fish, and fermented foods.
  • Avoid processed sugar, seed oils, alcohol, and refined carbs.
  • Experiment with gluten or dairy elimination if suspected triggers.

Joint-Friendly Movement

  • Do quad strengthening and hamstring stretches daily.
  • Use supportive shoes or orthotics for daily walking.
  • Rest and ice after activity. Avoid deep kneeling or squats early on.

Sleep & Stress Tools

  • Try 10 min of 4-7-8 breathing before bed.
  • Take magnesium glycinate or chamomile tea after dinner.
  • Use red light, meditation, or cold splash to activate vagus nerve.
