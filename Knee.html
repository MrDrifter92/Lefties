Your Functional Recovery Plan

🍽️ Diet & Inflammation

What you eat drives inflammation up or down. Load up on greens, omega-3s, and gut-friendly whole foods. Cut the crap — sugar, deep fried stuff, and foods that bloat or spike you. Trial gluten/dairy/nightshade elimination if needed. Hydrate like it’s a job.

🦵 Movement & Rehab

Move — but smart. Rehab is about loading without flaring. Avoid deep squats and long sitting. Strengthen quads, stretch hammies and IT band. Walk. Swim. Cycle gently. Don't push through sharp pain. Listen to the knee.

🧘 Sleep & Stress

Inflammation isn’t just food — it’s your nervous system too. Prioritise sleep, cut back overstimulation, and use breathwork or cold exposure to calm the vagus nerve. Track your mood. Keep your body out of fight-or-flight where healing can happen.

🧊 Recovery Toolkit

Your extras: magnesium, turmeric, collagen, epsom baths, red light therapy, massage, CBD cream. Trial one at a time. Use cold after movement, heat before. Find your rhythm — don’t throw everything at it at once. Track what helps.

📍 What Is a Plica?

A medial plica is a leftover fold in the inner knee. Most people never notice it. But when it thickens or gets irritated, it causes friction, catching, and pain — usually misdiagnosed as “patellofemoral pain” or “overuse.”

Your MRI

That black line on the scan? That’s the plica — and it shouldn’t be there. After 7.5 years, this is the first time someone spotted it. This changes the whole treatment approach.
