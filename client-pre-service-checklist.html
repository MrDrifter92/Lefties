Dashboard

just a few things..

A quick to-do list before we arrive. Ticking these off helps us get straight to the deep clean and gives you the best possible result.

Clear the Decks

Please move any small items and breakables out of the areas we'll be cleaning. Think things like pot plants, lamps, rugs, kid's toys, and pet beds.

A Quick Vacuum

Giving your carpets a quick vacuum beforehand is ideal. It gets the surface-level fluff out of our way so our deep-clean can focus on the embedded stuff.

Plan for Pets & Kids

For everyone's safety, please make sure children and pets are secured in a separate area, away from the cleaning zone and our equipment.

Access & Parking

We'll need access to a tap and a power outlet. Please also ensure there's a clear parking spot for our van as close to the entry as possible.

A Note on Furniture

We can generally work around or carefully move larger items like lounges and coffee tables. We don't move very heavy or delicate items (pianos, bookcases). If you have any concerns, just ask us when we arrive.

Thanks for your help! We'll see you soon.
