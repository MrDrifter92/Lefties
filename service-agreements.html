Residential Agreement

Thanks for trusting us to bring your place back to life. This isn't a scary legal contract—it's just us being upfront about how we work, what we need from you, and what you can expect from the job.

1. The Job We'll Do

We agree to perform the carpet and/or upholstery cleaning services we quoted you for. We’ll use our professional equipment, the right chemicals for the job, and our best skills to get the best possible result. Our goal is a spotless revival, every time.

2. What We Need From You

  • Access: A clear path for us to bring our gear in and out.
  • Space: Please move any small furniture, breakables, toys, and personal items from the areas we'll be cleaning. We can help with larger items like sofas and tables, but we can't move heavy or delicate things like pianos, entertainment units, or antique furniture.
  • Power & Water: Access to a standard power outlet and a tap (preferably an outdoor one).
  • Safety: For their safety, please keep kids and pets in another part of the house or secured away from the work area and our equipment.

3. The Price

The price is what we agreed on in your quote. If we get here and see something that needs a different approach or extra work (like a major stain you forgot to mention), we'll stop and talk to you about it first. No surprise charges, ever.

4. Paying the Bill

For residential jobs, payment is due on the day, as soon as the job is done. We accept cash, debit, or credit card on the spot. We’ll give you a receipt right then and there.

5. The Reality of Stains & Damage

We're good, but we're not magicians.

  • No Guarantee: We'll do everything we can, but we can't guarantee 100% removal of all stains. Some things—like bleach spots, old dye stains, or pet urine that’s been there for ages—can be permanent. We'll be honest with you about what to expect before we start.
  • Pre-existing Conditions: Sometimes, cleaning can reveal existing damage that was hidden by dirt, like worn-out fibres, sun fading, or delamination (where the carpet backing separates). We are not responsible for pre-existing issues that become visible after a proper clean.
  • DIY Spotters: If you've used a supermarket spot cleaner or tried a home remedy, please let us know. These chemicals can react with our professional products and affect the final result.

6. A Heads-Up on Certain Fibres

Every carpet is different. Natural fibres like wool and cotton need special care. We are trained to clean them safely, but there's always a small risk of things like minor shrinkage or colour variation. We manage this by using wool-safe, pH-neutral products and proper techniques.

7. What Happens If Something Goes Wrong

We treat your home like it's our own. In the unlikely event that we cause any damage, please tell us immediately. Our liability is limited to the cost of the cleaning service provided.

By letting us start work, you agree to these straightforward terms. Let's get this revival started.

Commercial Agreement

Thanks for choosing Lefties to keep your commercial space in top shape. This document outlines our working agreement so we're all on the same page. It’s all about clear expectations and a great result.

1. The Job We'll Do

We agree to perform the carpet and/or upholstery cleaning services we quoted you for. We’ll use our professional equipment, the right chemicals for the job, and our best skills to get the best possible result.

2. What We Need From You

  • Access: Clear and safe access to the areas to be cleaned, as agreed upon.
  • Space: Areas should be clear of items like documents, electronics, and personal effects.
  • Power & Water: Access to a standard power outlet and water supply.
  • Safety: The work area must be clear of your staff and customers for the duration of the clean for safety reasons.

3. The Price

The price is as per our accepted quote. If the scope of work changes, we will issue a variation for your approval before proceeding. No surprise charges, ever.

4. Invoicing & Payment Terms

We get that businesses run on invoices. Our payment terms are based on the type of work we’re doing for you:

  • Standard Commercial Jobs: For one-off or infrequent cleans, we operate on a 7-day account. The invoice will be sent upon job completion and is payable within 7 calendar days.
  • Major Contracts & Ongoing Service: For clients with a formal, ongoing service agreement (like aged care facilities, hotels, or large offices), we offer 30-day account terms. Invoices are typically issued at the end of each month and are payable within 30 calendar days.

Late payments may be subject to a surcharge as outlined in your quote. All quotes must be formally accepted before work can be scheduled.

5. The Reality of Stains & Damage

  • No Guarantee: While we aim for the best results, we cannot guarantee 100% removal of all stains, especially those of a permanent nature (e.g., dyes, bleach, long-term chemical spills).
  • Pre-existing Conditions: We are not responsible for pre-existing conditions such as fibre wear, fading, or backing separation that may become more apparent after cleaning.

6. What Happens If Something Goes Wrong

We operate with full care and professionalism. In the unlikely event of damage caused by our team, please notify us immediately. Our liability is limited to the cost of the cleaning service provided.

By accepting our quote and allowing us to commence work, your business agrees to these straightforward terms. We look forward to working with you.
