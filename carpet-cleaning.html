Carpet Revival

Deep extraction clean for rentals, families, and “what happened here?” moments across Hervey Bay.

What’s included

  • Inspection & fibre check
  • Pre-spray + agitation on traffic lanes
  • Hot water extraction / steam clean
  • Stain attempts included (no miracle claims)
  • Deodorise & neutralise

Good to know

  • Drying: 4–8 hours (windows/fans help)
  • Furniture: light items moved; no pianos/fish tanks
  • Pet odours: may need enzyme treatment (extra)
  • Wool vs nylon: method adjusted to suit fibre

Aftercare

Keep foot traffic light until dry. Place towels under metal/wooden legs for 24hrs. Ventilate rooms.
