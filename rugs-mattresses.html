Rug & Mattress Cleaning

Extend the life and freshness of your rugs and mattresses with Lefties Carpet Cleaning. Beyond just surface cleaning, our specialized services delve deep to remove embedded dirt, dust mites, allergens, and odors, ensuring a truly hygienic environment. From delicate area rugs to large mattresses, we use safe, non-toxic products and proven methods to protect your investments and promote a healthier living space.

  • Pre-inspection and material assessment: We begin with a thorough inspection of your rug or mattress to identify its material, construction, and any specific areas of concern like stains or heavy soiling. This allows us to select the most appropriate and gentle cleaning method.
  • Deep vacuuming for dry soil removal: Using powerful, specialized vacuums, we meticulously remove dry particulate matter, dust mites, pet dander, and other allergens embedded deep within the fibers. This crucial step prevents these particles from becoming mud during the wet cleaning process.
  • Targeted pre-treatment: Any identified stains or heavily soiled areas are pre-treated with eco-friendly, non-toxic solutions specifically formulated for rugs and mattresses. These solutions work to break down and loosen dirt and stains, preparing them for effective extraction.
  • Gentle cleaning and extraction: Depending on the material, we employ either a low-moisture hot water extraction system for a deep clean or a delicate dry cleaning method for sensitive rugs. Our process effectively removes dirt, allergens, and odors without over-wetting, protecting the integrity of your items.
  • Hygienic sanitation and deodorization: Beyond just cleaning, we focus on hygiene. Our process includes steps to sanitize the surface, eliminating bacteria and common allergens. We also apply professional deodorizers to neutralize odors, leaving your rugs and mattresses smelling fresh and clean.
  • Accelerated drying: We utilize specialized air movers and techniques to significantly reduce drying times. This minimizes the risk of mold or mildew growth and ensures your rugs and mattresses are ready for use as quickly as possible, typically within a few hours.

How long does it take to dry?

Drying times for rugs and mattresses can vary based on material, thickness, and environmental conditions. Generally, most items are dry within **4 to 8 hours**. We use high-velocity air movers and ensure proper ventilation to expedite the process, minimizing any inconvenience and ensuring your items are ready for comfort as soon as possible.
